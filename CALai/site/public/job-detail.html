Senior Frontend Developer

TechNova • San Francisco, Hybrid

About the Role

TechNova is looking for a Senior Frontend Developer to lead our core product expansion. You will be joining a high-impact team dedicated to building intuitive, high-performance interfaces that simplify complex professional workflows. As a senior member of the team, you will influence our architectural decisions and mentor junior developers.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain high-performance web applications using React and TypeScript.
  • Collaborate with designers and product managers to translate complex requirements into seamless user experiences.

  • Champion best practices in testing, documentation, and code reviews.

Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in frontend engineering with a strong portfolio of React-based projects.
  • Mastery of modern CSS (Tailwind, CSS Modules) and architectural patterns.
  • Deep understanding of browser internals, performance profiling, and accessibility (WCAG).
  • Experience with GraphQL and state management libraries (Redux, Recoil).
